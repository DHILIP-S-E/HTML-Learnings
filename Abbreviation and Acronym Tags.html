Abbreviation

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was an amazing scientist and the 11th President of India. He was born in 1931 and passed away in 2015. He helped develop India's missile program and was known as the "Missile Man." He believed in the power of education and worked to inspire young people. As president, he focused on education and youth empowerment. His legacy continues to inspire many people

Acronym

ISRO is the space agency of the Government of India, responsible for the country's space research and exploration programs. Established in 1969, ISRO has achieved significant milestones in space technology, including satellite launches, lunar exploration, and Mars missions.
