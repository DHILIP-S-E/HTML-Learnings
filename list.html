    Chicken 65 is a popular Indian appetizer known for its spicy and flavorful taste. Here's a simple recipe to make Chicken 65:

  • 500g boneless chicken pieces, cut into bite-sized cubes
  • 2 tablespoons yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour or all-purpose flour
